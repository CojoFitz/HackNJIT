Cat Facts:

Cats are domesticated carnivorous mammals that are valued by humans for their companionship and rodent hunting abilities. They are thought to have been domesticated in regions along the Eastern Mediterranean Sea around 7500-7200 BC. Cats are known for their quick reflexes, sharp teeth, retractable claws, and strong senses. Cats benefit from their strong smelling abilities and natural night vision. They are lovely animals and are furry friends to many humans around the world.
